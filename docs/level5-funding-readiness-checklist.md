# Level 5 Funding Readiness Checklist

This checklist addresses QA feedback that the funding step needed clearer pre-submit guidance.

Before funding a StudyBond case, reviewers should verify:

- The student wallet address matches the onboarded student profile.
- The payer wallet address is present when a parent or guardian funds the case.
- The verifier wallet address is the institution, agency, dorm operator, or landlord reviewer.
- The mediator wallet address is separate from the student and verifier wallets.
- The case amount, asset code, release condition, refund condition, and expiry date are visible before signing.
- The app is connected to Stellar testnet, not mainnet.
- The funding transaction hash is captured after submission and appears in the wallet proof table.

This file is proof material only; it does not replace the in-app validation or smart contract checks.
