StudyBond: problem and solution

International students and families coordinate proof-of-funds and conditional deposits across institutions.

StudyBond makes roles, funding, evidence, release, refund, and disputes visible in one Stellar workflow.

Market opportunity

Education deposits, housing deposits, and visa-related proof-of-funds workflows are large, fragmented trust problems.

Architecture

Next.js + Prisma/Neon → Soroban escrow → Freighter/Rabet → public verification and analytics.

Growth strategy

Acquire through institutions and agencies; use feedback to reduce funding friction and improve reviewer confidence.

Future roadmap

Add institution integrations, configurable policies, notifications, and a production compliance layer.
