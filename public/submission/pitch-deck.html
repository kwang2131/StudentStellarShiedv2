Problem02 / 12

Education deposits carry high stakes and low visibility.

Student

Needs a clear funding path and evidence that the deposit is protected.

Family

Needs confidence about where funds are held and who can release them.

Institution

Needs a reviewable proof-of-funds and approval trail.

When funding, evidence, release, and refund decisions are split across messages and payment tools, each party loses context.

Solution03 / 12

Turn a conditional deposit into a shared workflow.

01

Create bond

02

Fund safely

Connect a Stellar wallet with visible network context.

03

Submit evidence

Give reviewers the proof they need in one place.

04

Resolve outcome

Release, refund, or dispute with role-based actions.
